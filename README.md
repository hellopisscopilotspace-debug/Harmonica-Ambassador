## 👁 The Concept
The world is tired of aggressive manipulation. **Harmonica-Ambassador** is an interface layer that transforms "predatory ads" into "honest offers." It is designed for the transition period where businesses and users agree on a fair exchange of attention for dividends.

## 🧬 Key Features
- **Visual Deceleration:** Automatically softens ad visuals, removing high-stress colors and fonts.
- **Linguistic Sanitization:** Replaces manipulative triggers ("SHOCK", "BUY NOW") with neutral, respectful terms.
- **Dividend Tracking:** A transparent log of benefits (discounts, tokens, access) received by the user for viewing the content.

"You give me your honest proposal — I give you my calm attention. We both win."
